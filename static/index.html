Hypixel Forum Alerts

Privacy Policy | Source

Hypixel Forum Alerts is an unofficial Chrome (and soon to be other browsers) extension that will give you notifications on your desktop for new unread alerts or conversations on the Hypixel Forums.

Unfortunately, only Chrome is available right now, but other browsers will be coming in the future.

Unaffiliated with the Hypixel Network.

Features

  • Receive notifications on your desktop whenever you get an alert or conversation on the Hypixel Forums.
  • Complete control: Ability to toggle on only the notifications you want to receive.

Planned Features:

  • Receive notifications whenever a thread is posted to the front page.
  • Receive notifications when either Hypixel or Mojang sessions go offline.
  • Toggleable ability to receive all these notifications via /msg in-game.
